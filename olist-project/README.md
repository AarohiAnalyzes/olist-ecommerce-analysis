# Olist Brazilian E-commerce Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
Comprehensive exploratory and business analysis of the Brazilian e-commerce marketplace dataset.  
The project focuses on uncovering insights related to **sales performance**, **customer behavior**, **product dynamics**, and **seller strategies**.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions

1. **Sales Performance**  
   - How does monthly revenue evolve over time?  
   - Are there seasonal patterns or anomalies?

2. **Customer Analysis**  
   - Where are customers geographically concentrated?  
   - Which states drive the most revenue?

3. **Product & Seller Insights**  
   - Which product categories dominate revenue?  
   - How do seller business models differ?  
   - Does price influence customer satisfaction?

## 🔍 Key Findings

✔ Marketplace dominated by single-item orders (1.13 items/order)  
✔ Strong revenue seasonality (peak in Nov 2017 – Black Friday effect)  
✔ Southeast Brazil drives majority of customers & revenue  
✔ Top categories account for large revenue concentration  
✔ Seller strategies vary (premium vs volume models)  
✔ **Price vs Review Score Correlation:** ~0 → Price does not drive satisfaction
